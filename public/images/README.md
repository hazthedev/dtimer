## Photography Guidelines

- **Style:** Warm, natural lighting. Steam and motion are good.
- **Mood:** Generous portions, family-style, "rasa rumah"
- **Avoid:** Overly styled, minimalist, or dark-moody aesthetics
- **Resolution:** Minimum 1200px wide for hero shots, 800px for cards
- **Format:** JPG or WebP
